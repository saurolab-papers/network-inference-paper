Follicular fluid model of the Article Bungay2006 (blood plasma model also available in MODEL1105090001). It is a modification of Bungay2003 (MODEL9852292468). Factors are denoted by their numbers, with _l and _f suffixes depending on whether they are lipid-bound or fluid phase.

Thrombin generation curve in follicular fluid as depicted by figure 3a of the article. Model integrated and simulated in Copasi 4.6.32, plotted with Matplotlib.
